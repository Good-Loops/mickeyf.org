    Class Mandelbrot

    GPU Mandelbrot animation implementation.

    Lifecycle:

    • init is called once with a PIXI Application.
    • step is called once per frame.
    • updateConfig may be called at any time to patch config.
    • scheduleDisposal / startDisposal drive teardown; dispose releases PIXI resources.
